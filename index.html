  1. Handicrafts - The art of expressing one's tradition and culture using hand in a unique and artistic way. Our artisans are skilled and express their traditional know hows through their art. Some of our Handicrafts are Clay produce, Wood works, Jaipur blue pottery.

    2. Clay produce

    Clay pottery and objects, which are also referred to as "artifacts" in archeological language are exclusively shaped with love and baked like a sweet bread by our artisans. We use acrylic paint to finish the product which can be customized. These range from traditonal Indian masks to candle stands to simple decoratives at home and many more.

    Wood works

    The wood peckers in our industry leave behind no shred in turning a mere log of wood into art which can be preserved and cherished. The finished product, from individual sculptures to hand-worked mouldings composing jewellery boxes, famous rajasthani puppets, book holders and many more. Wood is durable and stays termite-free.

    Jaipur blue pottery

    Pantine named Classic Blue as officially the Color of the Year 2020. “Instilling calm, confidence, and connection, this enduring blue hue highlights our desire for a dependable and stable foundation on which to build as we cross the threshold into a new era”.

  2. Quinoa - With the on going demand and market we bring to you good quality organic white quinoa produced with all care on our mother earth. It is grown naturally and helps you fight all those baddies in the body. We have white and red ones. So, are you ready to add this lovely lady to your diet? Let us tell you a small secret! She goes with everything.

    3. Quinoa

    Quinoa is usually considered a whole grain, but it’s actually a seed that you can cook up just like a whole grain such as barley or rice. It has a similar nutritional profile to brown rice but with slightly higher protein content. For all the vegans and vegetarians out there, this is a boon in disguise.

  3. Red Chilli - For all those who like “small but hot”, we have the perfect lady for you with a different heat in every bite of her. Someone without whom the kitchen and your tongues feel bland. India, home to one of the spiciest red chilly peppers has been in the flavor game for centuries now. As you come down south she gets spicier and hotter, exactly how we like her. So do our mothers.

  4. Chia Seeds - Chia seeds, AKA Salvia hispanica belong to mint family. They transform into these jelly balloons who are 10-12 times original size when soaked in water which is nothing but all the goodness. The European Union approved chia seeds as a novel food. They can be crushed, ground, made into bread, used instead of egg, as a sweetener, can be used instead of milk and many more.

    5. Chia Seeds
